Video Entertainment

Youtube
Youtube is a free video streaming website, that offers content uploaded by individual users, and is one of the most popular video streaming websites on the internet.
Twitch Tv
Twitch.tv is a popular live streaming website, that's most popularly used to stream video games, and offers a variety of different content, along with different content creators.
Netflix
Netflix is a video streaming website, that offers a variety of different movies to choose from. You must sign up, and add Netflix to your subscription in order to watch movies and stream them online.

Video Gaming

League of Legends
League of Legends is the most popular online game around the world, It is a moba genre game, where players are set into an arena, and battle it out to victory, allowing only one team to succeed in the end.
Fortnite: Battle Royale
Fortnite: Battle Royale, is a free video game, battle royale genre game, where players are thrown into an arena, and the last man standing wins.
Minecraft
Minecraft is an open world survival game, where players have the ability to endlessly create whatever their minds are capable of imagining.

Social Media

Instagram
Instagram is an Internetbased photosharing application and service that allows users to share pictures and videos.

Snapchat is a mobile social media application, that allows users to share pictures that will self distruct after a pre-set amount of time.
Twitter
Twitter is an online news and social networking service where users post and interact with messages, known as "tweets."
